<h2> What does the people in Melbourne do for a living?</h2>
<p>
The job market can tell a lot about a city. The two biggest industries in Melbourne are Financial and Insurance services and Professional, Scientific and Technical services. Melbourne is home for top technical companies such as Seek and Rea group. ANZ bank, one of the country’s four biggest banks, is also located here in Melbourne. It is also worth noting that there is an industry for mining in the city, one of the largest mining companies in the world, BHP Billiton mining, is in Melbourne. 
</p>
